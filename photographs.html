Retrospective: Watching out the steps.

Naturalism: Humans, like all living beings, consciousness arises from the brain.

Coronavirus: Isolation and connection; visiting the invisible enemy (Picasso Exhibition, Houston 2021).

Blue Tunnel: Time, the inevitability of mortality, and the solitary nature of human existence.

Contemplation vs. Loneliness: An exile, a wanderer, or someone abandoned?

Red Light: Directionless, moving mindlessly under artificial light.

Chromotherapy: Who we trust, who we desire, who we fear.

Two Cyclists: Bridges are weird, right? Like, we’re technically suspended over nothing.

Silent Reciprocity: The owner’s voice, the dog’s silence—both understood.

Reflections Unseen: Perfect! Nobody will even know it’s us.

Photography Whispering: "Me too!"

Mid-Crossing Confessions: Somewhere, a traffic camera is weeping.

The quiet rebellion of stillness: Refusing to perform joy on demand, fully!

Parallel Solitude: Togetherness and loneliness sharing the same bedrock!

Sublime: For a long moment, neither speaks. Vastness exposes unspoken things.
